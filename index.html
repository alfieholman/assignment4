West Norfolk Swimming Club are excited to announce that we will be welcoming new members to the club from 12th April.

Trials will be running in a Covid secure environment throughout April and May for new swimmers to join our team. If you are swimming in Stage 6 or above, are aged between 7 and 11, love swimming and are keen to improve then we can help you. We have squads for a variety of ages and abilities and there are opportunities for swimmers who would like to compete and those that just swim for fitness. If you’re interested in joining the WNSC family then email headcoach@westnorfolksc.co.uk to arrange your free trial session #purplearmy
West Norfolk Swimming Club has gained a strong reputation over many years for developing young swimmers through professional and structured coaching in a competitive and supportive environment. Our programme aims to maximise the potential of each individual swimmer and help them become the best they can be whilst enjoying the process of learning and improving in and around the pool. It is designed to ensure swimmers are ready to perform and excel in a competitive environment be that at Club, County, Regional or National level.
